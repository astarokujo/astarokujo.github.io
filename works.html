The Science Behind It

Studies show that all objects emit some kind of infrared radiation and our scientists were able to find the exact radiation that COVID-19 emits in order to create our patented technology. The Covid detector works similar to Thermography in the sense that the cameras detect patients' temperature by recognizing and capturing different levels of infrared light. This light is invisible to the naked eye, but can be felt as heat if the intensity is high enough.

Lens

The Covindicator Goggles are also equipped with industry revolutionary lens technology. With this new technology the goggles automatically begin to scan every user's eyes as soon as they put it on and determine their vision. After that process is completed the goggles then auto converts the lens to give every user 20/20 vision. The goggles also come with UV protection and an option to zoom in and out in order to see further away!

Lung Detection's Never Been Easier

Inside of a Covid lung detector camera, there are a bunch of tiny measuring devices that capture infrared radiation, called microbolometers, and each pixel has one. From there, the microbolometer records the temperature and then assigns that pixel to the appropriate color.

Virus Detection

Our goggles can scan the capacity of an entire city using its cutting edge detection technology. It focuses its scanners within a certain proximity and detects whether or not other people/objects within its radius are infected with the virus. If detected, they will be depicted into our system’s GPS.

We Respect Your Privacy

We allow our customers to have a say in data protection. Goggle users detected with the virus will immediately be notified that their information is stored in the system and will be asked if they wish to limit the data that’s displayed to other users before it gets sent to them. As for non-goggle users, they will only be noted as an unknown person carrying the virus to others.
