What are the Covindicator Goggles?

Covindicator Goggles are the sole product that will make you feel 100% protected at all times from the deadly COVID-19 virus. The Goggles are equipped with our new patented COVID-19 detector technology that works like thermography to detect the virus in infected bodies and on all objects that that person might have touched.

But that's not all…

The goggles come with industry changing lens technology that will give every user the perfect vision with the same pair of lenses! The Covindicator Goggles also comes with an app that pings all the people infected within 150ft of you. If you ever want to know whether the room you're about to go into has anyone that is infected with COVID-19, all you have to do is open up your app and wait for the pings. If there are no pings then you are in the clear - it’s as simple as that!
